Why Choose Us

About Our Elementary School

Dr. Alfredo Pio De Roda Elementary School is dedicated to nurturing young learners from Kindergarten through Grade 6. We provide a foundation of excellence that prepares students for their educational journey ahead.

Academic Excellence

Age-appropriate curriculum designed to build strong foundations in literacy, numeracy, and critical thinking skills for elementary learners.

Caring Teachers

Passionate elementary educators who understand child development and create nurturing environments where young learners flourish.

Character Building

Focus on developing values, social skills, and emotional intelligence that prepare children for success in school and life.

Grade Levels

Elementary Programs

Comprehensive educational programs designed specifically for young learners from Kindergarten through Grade 6

Kindergarten

Play-based learning that introduces basic literacy, numeracy, and social skills in a nurturing environment

Grades 1-2

Building fundamental reading, writing, and math skills with hands-on learning experiences

Grades 3-4

Developing critical thinking and problem-solving skills across all subject areas with increased independence

Grades 5-6

Advanced elementary curriculum preparing students for middle school with leadership opportunities

Enrichment Programs

Arts & Creativity

Music, visual arts, and creative expression programs that nurture artistic talents

Sports & Fitness

Physical education and sports programs promoting healthy lifestyle and teamwork

STEM Learning

Science, technology, engineering, and math programs that encourage exploration and discovery
