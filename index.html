Dr. Alfredo Pio De Roda Elementary School

Welcome to Dr. Alfredo Pio De Roda Elementary School — a place where young minds are nurtured, values are instilled, and futures are built. Established with a mission to provide quality education for all, our school stands as a beacon of learning, integrity, and excellence.

Through the years, our teachers and students have worked hand-in-hand to foster an environment of respect, curiosity, and continuous growth. We believe that every child deserves an opportunity to shine, and we are committed to helping them reach their full potential.

We take pride in our rich history, dedicated faculty, and our ever-evolving programs designed to meet the needs of a modern world — all while staying true to the Filipino values that define us.

Together, we build not just knowledge — but character, compassion, and community.

About Us

History: Founded to serve the heart of the community, Dr. Alfredo Pio De Roda Elementary School has been a pillar of quality education for generations.

Mission: To provide accessible, inclusive, and values-based learning to every child.

Vision: A community of learners who embody integrity, compassion, and knowledge.
